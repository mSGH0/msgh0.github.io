Introduction

There is a quality of life we possess as the new creation that goes beyound the natural bios life or the psuche life in the soul. This life we poaswss as new creation is primarily expressed as the divine life of God known as zoe.

Zoe is a generic word that connotes spiritual life; a life that exists in God. In John 3:16 the Greek word translated life gives an upgraded version of the life God givea to us; Zoe-aion (where aion means ages)

Eternal Life Zoe-aion, is the life of the ages to come.It is not just thw life in God but one that is consistent with wvery age that is not according to thw dimensions of time.It is uncreated life.

Ageless life, which is eternal life, means a life that is applicable in every age. Every possibilty that exist within the perception of creation is a preview of certain age within which that potential would become normal.

Foundational Fact About Eternal Life

1. Eternal Life begins now; after death(1 John 5:12; John 5:24): Eternal life is not something you enter after death.

2. Eternal Life is not a duration but dimension eternal life is quality not duration( 1 John 5 : 11- 13)

3. Eternal life is the foundation of a believer's supernatural walk.

4. Eternal life emphasizes rules(Ezekiel 36:26 - 27, Gal 2:20)

5. Eternal life has its own intelligence(Romans 8:11)

The Revelation Of Divine Participation

Believers don't just initiate God - we participate in Him. Jesus don't come to make better men. He came to make new men.

Participation produce expression. Because by nature, we can:

  1. Think divine thoughts
  2. Express divine wisdom
  3. Walk in divine authority
  4. Function in divine aurhority
  5. Manifest divine love
  6. Operate with divine power

The Protocols of Life Transference

Eternal life is not a substance; it is a transferable economy.

Three Protocols Governing The Transfer:

1. The Protocol Of Union(John 17:21): Life flows through oness. Whatever you unite with you derive energy from.

2. The Protocol of Submission(Hebrews 7:7): the inferior recieves life from superior authority. Spiritual authority structures are life distribution systems.

3. The Protocol of Alignment(Romans 8:7): Life flows where order is maintained like blood flowing through the arteries. Where rebellion exists flow is restricted.
