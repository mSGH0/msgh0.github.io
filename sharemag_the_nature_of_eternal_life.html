Introduction

There is a quality of life we possess as the new creation that goes beyound the natural bios life or the psuche life in the soul. This life we possess as new creation is primarily expressed as the divine life of God known as zoe.

Zoe is a generic word that connotes spiritual life; a life that exists in God. In John 3:16 the Greek word translated life gives an upgraded version of the life God givea to us; Zoe-aion (where aion means ages)

Eternal Life Zoe-aion, is the life of the ages to come. It is not just the life in God but one that is consistent with every age that is not according to the dimensions of time. It is uncreated life.

Ageless life, which is eternal life, means a life that is applicable in every age. Every possibility of creation is a preview of a certain age within which that potential would become normal.

A person's life determines the possibilities that exist in their realm. There is life in God that is responsible for every possibility in Him and define what prevails in His realm. So the life in God defines the possiblities that are available to God to exercise.

The kind of life that runs through you determines the possibilities that exist for you. When you become conscious of the life that runs through you, you can now explore the possibilities that exist for you.

Everything you know is in God is actually available to you because the same life that is in God is in you, but it is your consciousness that determines the extent to which you can explore the possibilities of the God-life in you.

Foundational Fact About Eternal Life

1. Eternal Life is not a Duration but Dimension Eterrnal life is quality not duration(1 John 5 : 11 - 13)

2. Eternal life begins now; after death. Eternal life is not something you enter after the death - 1 John 5 : 12, John 5 : 24

3. Eternal life is the foundation of the believers supernatural walk.

4. Eternal Life emphasizes nature, Religion emphasizes rules - Ezekiel 36:26 - 27, Galations 2.20.

5. Eternal life has its own intelligence - Romans 8 : 11.

The Revelation Of Divine Participation

Believers Don't Just imitate God - We Participate in Him. Jesus didn't come to make better men. He came to make new men.

Participation Produces Expression. Because we share divine nature, we can:

  • Think divine thoughts
  • Express divine wisdom
  • Walk in divine authority
  • Function in divine authority
  • Manifest divine love
  • Operate divine power

The Protocols of Life Transference

Eternal life is not only a substance;It is a transferable economy.

Three protocols governing the transfer:

1. The Protocol of Union - John 17 : 21. Life flows through oneness. Whatever you unite with , you derive energy from.

2. The Protocol of Submission - Hebrews 7 : 7. The inferior receives life from the superior. Spiritual authority structures are life distribution systems.

3. The Protocol of Alignment. Romans 8 : 7. Life flows where order is maintained like blood flowing through the arteries. Where rebellion exists flow is restricted.
