Introduction - The Realms Of Operation Of The Eternal Life

The word realm is the same mamlaka (Hebrew), Greek (Basilia), which means jurisdiction. Realm defines space and domain. Realm establishes an overarching space, and within that space are found dimensions – and dimensions are defined by levels.

There are realms of operation in God, and inside those realms are dimensions, and levels within which the dimensions are expressed. Dimension has to do with depth, whilst realm has to do with atmosphere/space.

There are realms in the operation of eternal life and dimensions of eternal life. Inside Christ is a realm, and that realm expresses a certain jurisdictional authority.

The Realms Of Operation Of Eternal Life.

Eternal life operates in the life of the new creation in three realms:

1) Realm of Revelatory Intelligence –Life as light (John 1:4).

Eternal life generates spiritual intelligence by bringing a person into revelation, discernment, prophetic perception, insight into mysteries, and supernatural clarity.

2) The Realm of Vitality and Strength – Life as energy

The life of the flesh requires food to produce energy, but the life of the spirit is an energy in itself to produce a certain vitality in the body.

By the life of energy in the realm of vitality, we are able to generate stamina for prayer. It produces vitality as health in the body. Colossians 1:29

3) Realm of Deathlessness – Life as Immortality. 2 Timothy 1:10

The Dimensions Of Operation Of The Eternal Life.

There are three dimensions to the operations of eternal life:

1) The Biological Dimension of Eternal Life

Eternal life has its own biological structure.

a) Eternal Life Has Genetic Properties – 1 Peter 1:23 (We see the word seed being sperma – genetic code.)

This means:

  • Eternal life rewrites spiritual DNA
  • You carry God’s genetic essence
  • Your spirit has divine coding for God-like expressions – victory, dominion, power, etc.

Eternal life is an ongoing reality where we keep growing in Christ and keep building on the capability of that life.

b) Eternal Life Has Immunological Properties – Romans 8:2

The law functions like spiritual immunity:

  • resisting corruption in the body
  • resisting decay
  • resisting demonic infestation
  • resisting moral breakdown
  • resisting soul fragmentation

c) Eternal Life Quickens the Mortal Body

2) The Metaphysical Dimension of Eternal Life

Metaphysics deals with realities beyond physical perception.

a) Eternal Life Exists as a Dimension of Life in Christ – 1 John 5:11–12

b) Eternal Life Exists as a Law – Romans 8:2

Three metaphysical implications:

  • It is predictable
  • It is enforceable
  • It is superior to all other laws

c) Eternal Life Exists as Energy

The Eschatological Dimension Of The Eternal Life

1) Eternal Life Will Eventually Swallow Up Immortality – 1 Corinthians 15:54

2) Eternal Life Is the Seed of Resurrection - 1 Thessalonians 4:16; Romans 8:11 The same life that raised Christ is in the believer now.

3) Eternal Life Guarantees Immortality in the Age to Come – Ephesians 2:4–8; Luke 18:29
